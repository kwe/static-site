Guest & Visitor Internet Wireless Service

We operate two systems for Guests and Visitors to access our Wireless and internet service.

1) Eduroam

All staff and students from most educational establishments can access the internet here at all campuses of the University of South Wales via the Eduroam service.

2) Guest Account

Any visiting individual unable to access “eduroam” can use our Guest Wireless Service. This service allows Internet access for short-term guests using their own mobile devices.

Requesting and using the Guest Account creation service at Pontypridd & Cardiff Campuses

Please note that a member of staff of this University must act as a 'sponsor’ for a visiting guest. Guest accounts can be created by Departmental IT Coordinators or alternatively any nominated member of staff can act as a 'sponsor’ and create guest accounts themselves (although this is a slightly longer process). Please contact IT Customer Support on +44 1443 4 82882 or via the Point of Business system if you wish to become a sponsor.

However, if you need to create a Guest Account please follow these steps:

  1. If this is the first time you are setting up a Guest account please contact IT Customer Support on +44 1443 4 82882 or via the Point of Business system and ask to be added as a sponsor for the Glamguest (USW Guest Wireless Network) account creation service.
  2. Create a sponsored guest account (via the web service) and print out the account details for the guest. Guest Accounts created prior to visiting can be emailed to the guest’s external email address.
  3. Provide advice and/or documentation using the appropriate USW Wireless Network Guides .
  4. Your guest can then to sign into their Guest Account and access the wireless network.

Requesting and using the Guest Account creation service at Newport & Caerleon Campuses

Please note that a member of staff of this University must act as a 'sponsor’ for the visiting guest.

Guest accounts can be created by Departmental IT advisors located in Caerleon (Rathmell E29) and Newport (Library desk).

You will be required to sign user’s terms & conditions.

If you require five accounts or more please contact IT Support on 01443 4 82882 or via the Point of Business system


For IT support at any campus please contact our Customer Support 'First Response’ team.
