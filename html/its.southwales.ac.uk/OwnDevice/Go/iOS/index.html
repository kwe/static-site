Getting started with your iOS device

Welcome to the getting started guide for your iOS device.

Below is a step by step guide which will walk you through the process of connecting to the Eduroam wireless service, on to configuring access to your email accounts and on to printing your work directly from your device (where available)

Connecting to the Wireless internet service.

The Eduroam service provides you with wireless network connections for your own equipment.

The service is now available at a number of University of South Wales locations, as displayed on our campus wireless map

This guide will show you how to connect to Eduroam wirelessly if you are using a mobile phone or other devices running iOS.

  • Note: The example devices in the following guide are iOS 5, but the instructions provided should be similar to those required for use with other versions of iOS 5.

If you would like to connect a computer with other operating systems, please contact IT Services Customer Support , or see our other Eduroam guides.

Step 1/3 – Before you start…

In order to access the Glamstudent network you must ensure that you have activated your university IT account and agree to be bound by our IT service terms and conditions whilst using our services.
IT account activation can be performed via the SSAA webpage: https://ssaa.southwales.ac.uk

Step 2/3 – Setting Up Your iOS device for use with Eduroam

  • Note: This guide assumes that your wireless adapter has already been installed and is known to be working. It is the responsibility of the user to configure and manage their own wireless equipment

Locate a suitable area where a wireless signal can be received (see our campus wireless map for details).

Load up your devices ‘Settings’, on our example, the settings icon in on the home screen.

Click on the Wifi settings option, then make sure Wi-Fi is switched ‘On’.

A list of available wireless networks should now be listed, if in an appropriate area Eduroam should be
listed, click on the Eduroam entry.

Step 3/3 – Network authentication

The device will now prompt you to enter your University username and password, after entering your
University username and password, please select the ‘Join’ button, this will initiate joining the
Eduroam wireless network.

Username: Please enter your University student id number followed by @southwales.ac.uk
Password: Enter your current University password

Your device should now be configured successfully for use with the Eduroam wireless network, in
order to start using the service, please load up Safari or your favourite web browser.

Configuring access to your email accounts on your iOS device.

To use your University e-mail on your mobile device, you first need to configure the device to connect
to the University e-mail servers.

  • Note: The example devices in the following guide are iOS 5, but the instructions provided should be similar to those required for use with other versions of iOS 5.

As the University student e-mail is run by Google, configuring the University email system is identical to setting up a Gmail account.

Before proceeding to configure your device, please ensure that it is currently connected to the internet either via WiFi, or by any 3g service offered by your mobile network operator, as your device will need to be able to connect to Gmail in order to complete the process successfully.

Once you have connected, on your iOS device, locate and load the ‘Mail’ application, as shown below:

You will now be prompted to select what type of email service you would like to use. To configure access to your student account, please select ‘Gmail’.

You will now be prompted to enter e-mail account details as follows:

Name : Please enter your full name
Email : Please enter your full University email address, i.e: 12345678@students.southwales.ac.uk
Password : Please enter your current University password
Description : Please enter ‘USW email’ or similar – this will allow you to easily distinguish what email account if you add/remove accounts in the future.
Upon completion – please click on the ‘Next’ button in the top right hand side of the screen.

Your mobile device will now sign in/verify your e-mail account details.

You will next prompted if you would like to synchronise your University ‘Calendar’ and ‘Notes’, by default ‘Mail’ is automatically selected.

Click ‘Save’ to complete the set up process.

Your University e-mail account should now be configured on your device, which should now begin syncronising your device with your accounts.
